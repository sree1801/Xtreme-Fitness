Track Your BMI

Xtreme Fitness is a workout tracking website that helps you track your BMI.
BMI is a useful tool for healthcare providers to assess risk factors for certain health conditions. BMI can't differentiate between body fat and muscle mass, so people with a lot of muscle bulk, like body builders, will have a high BMI but are not overweight.
